0: 'Effective Date',
1: 'Expense Report ID',
2: 'Expense Report Name',
3: 'Employee ID',
4: 'Employee Name',
5: 'Expense Payment Mode',
6: 'Tub',
7: 'Org',
8: 'Object',
9: 'Fund',
10: 'Activity',
11 'Subactivity',
12: 'Root',
13: 'Expense Type',
14: 'Business Purpose',
15: 'Expense Transaction Date',
16: 'CoA Allocated ($)']

Different lines of the same expense report will have the same Expense Report ID.
