Notes on the employee management system:

reports that employees have submitted. Each report has a unique ID, the employee's name, the date of submission, the total amount requested for reimbursement, listed by the UUID of the
expense report itself. The PI associated with the expense is listed in the
employee Name field; the HUID will be in the Employee ID field unless the expense is
charged to a group (like Privacy tools). Hypothesis is that
we will treat the project as a separate employee/PI, but that should
